Objective:
To win a point, eliminate the entire enemy team or capture the Control Point.
 
Other Notes:
Players become a ghost when they die, capable of healing allies by flying near them.

The last man standing on a team will be buffed.

The Control Point starts locked and unlocks later during each battle.

Once a point is scored for the respective team, all players will be respawned.

The first team to score 4 points wins.
